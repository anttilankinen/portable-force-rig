Portable Force Rig
A low-cost, open-source project to measure the bite forces of polymorphic leaf-cutter ants
Find out more

About

Leaf-cutter ants persist as a significant cause of crop damage. Existing measures to mitigate this damage, such as pesticides, often result in huge economic and environmental consequences. To develop better crop protection methods, it is vital to better understand the maximum bite force of leaf-cutter ants. This is why we developed a low-cost, open-source force rig capable of measuring their bite forces.
